Our Story

Located on one of the busiest streets in Calgary, Cafe 16 aims to serve quality drinks and desserts while offering a sweet hangout spot. To compliment our drinks and desserts, we have snacks inspired by Asian street eats.

Quality is our focus. We take pride in our milk teas because we believe in using quality ingredients. Our teas are brewed fresh every day and we use real milk rather than milk powder. Our waffles are cooked to order, using free-run chicken eggs.
